Full Week Schedule

Five days of tech, culture, sports, and celebration at PCU

Day 1 · Mon June 15
Monday, June 15, 2026

The Bezaleel Conference '26 & Inter-Departmental Debate

Kick off NACOS Week with The Bezaleel Conference — themed "Recalibrate: Craftsmanship Beyond the Four Walls of the School." Featuring industry speakers including the Founder of The Bezaleel Network, NACOS PCU President, and tech leaders. The day closes with a fierce Inter-Departmental Debate at the University Auditorium.

Conference · 10:00 AM Debate · 2:00 PM 📍 University Auditorium
Day 2 · Tue June 16
Tuesday, June 16, 2026 · Technology & Innovation Day

Panel Session, Demo Presentations & Movie Night 🎬

The biggest day of NACOS Week! Morning kicks off with a Technology & Innovation Panel Session, followed by exciting Demo Presentations where students showcase projects built to solve real campus problems. The day wraps up with a Movie Night at the FPAS Seminar Room.

Panel Session · Morning Demo Presentations · Afternoon Movie Night · 6:00 PM FPAS Seminar Room
Day 3 · Wed June 17
Wednesday, June 17, 2026

Sports Day ⚽🏀

Take a break from the screens and hit the field! NACOS Sports Day brings together students for friendly competitions across football, basketball, and more. Cheer on your classmates and show your team spirit.

⚽ Football 🏀 Basketball PCU Sports Complex 🏅 Prizes Available
Day 4 · Thu June 18
Thursday, June 18, 2026

Excursion to IITA Ibadan & Ventura 🌿

Step off campus and explore the world! NACOS takes students on an excursion to the International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (IITA) in Ibadan — one of Africa's leading research centres — followed by a visit to Ventura for fun and recreation.

🌿 IITA Ibadan Visit 🎡 Ventura Recreation 📍 Off Campus
Day 5 · Fri June 19
Friday, June 19, 2026

Award/Dinner Night 🏆✨

Close out NACOS Week in style! The Award Dinner Night celebrates outstanding students, competition winners, and the best demo presentations of the week. Dress up, enjoy great food, and celebrate the end of an unforgettable week.

Award Ceremony Dinner Closing Celebration Smart Casual Dress
